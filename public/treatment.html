Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about pond and lake treatment methods

Are chemical treatments safe for fish and wildlife?

Yes, when properly applied by licensed professionals. We use EPA-approved products at appropriate rates and timing to minimize impact on non-target species.

How long do biological treatments take to work?

Biological treatments typically show results within 2-4 weeks, with full effectiveness achieved over several months as the ecosystem adjusts.

Do I need permits for fish stocking?

Yes, fish stocking requires proper permits. We handle all permitting requirements and ensure compliance with state regulations.

How often should treatments be applied?

Treatment frequency depends on your specific situation. We develop customized maintenance schedules based on your water body's needs.

Can treatments be combined?

Yes, we often recommend integrated approaches combining mechanical, chemical, and biological methods for the best long-term results.
