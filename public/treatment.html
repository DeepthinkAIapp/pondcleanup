Treatment FAQs

Common questions about our treatment methods and processes

Are chemical treatments safe for fish and wildlife?

Yes, we use EPA-approved treatments that are specifically designed to target unwanted vegetation while being safe for fish, wildlife, and beneficial aquatic plants. Our application methods ensure minimal impact on the ecosystem.

How long do treatments take to show results?

Chemical treatments typically show visible results within 1-2 weeks, while biological treatments work more gradually over 4-6 weeks. The full benefits of treatment programs are usually evident within 2-3 months.

Do I need to stay out of the water after treatment?

Swimming and recreational use restrictions vary by treatment type and are clearly communicated before application. Most treatments have minimal restrictions, typically 24-48 hours for swimming.

How often do treatments need to be applied?

Treatment frequency depends on your specific conditions, goals, and the treatment method used. We develop customized maintenance schedules that typically range from monthly to annually, depending on your needs.

Can treatments be combined with mechanical removal?

Absolutely! Combining chemical/biological treatments with mechanical removal often provides the best long-term results. This integrated approach addresses both immediate vegetation issues and prevents future regrowth.
