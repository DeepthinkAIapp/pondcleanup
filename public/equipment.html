Truxor T50 Capabilities

Four core functions for comprehensive pond and lake management

Cut

Hydraulic cutting tools for applications both on land and on the water. Cattails, aquatic weeds, grasses, reeds, cane, lotus, lilies! Take it all down on land and in the water.

Cutting Applications:

  • Aquatic vegetation cutting and removal
  • Shoreline vegetation management
  • Invasive species control
  • Selective plant trimming
  • Precision cutting in tight spaces

Benefits:

  • Efficient vegetation removal
  • Minimal disturbance to water
  • Precise cutting control
  • Reduced regrowth

Collect

Grapple, baskets, and bucket help make the Truxor the best machine on the water. Seamless operation on land and water leveraging interchangeable tools to collect, clear, clean and remove vegetation and debris.

Collection Tools:

  • Hydraulic grapple for large debris
  • Collection baskets for vegetation
  • Bucket attachments for sediment
  • Floating debris collection
  • Shoreline cleanup tools

Benefits:

  • Complete debris removal
  • Efficient material handling
  • Versatile collection methods
  • Clean work areas

Dig & Dredge

Dredging equipment offers another level of performance and usability, pumping mud, muck and sediment without draining. Perfect for precision dredging of ponds, lakes, wetlands, fish or duck camps.

Dredging Applications:

  • Sediment removal and management
  • Pond bottom restoration
  • Channel deepening and maintenance
  • Muck and organic material removal
  • Precision dredging in sensitive areas

Benefits:

  • No water drainage required
  • Precision dredging control
  • Minimal environmental impact
  • Improved water depth and quality

Aeration

Complete water quality management with professional aeration systems for healthy pond and lake ecosystems. Our aeration solutions improve oxygen levels, reduce algae growth, and maintain balanced aquatic environments.

Aeration Benefits:

  • Improved water quality and clarity
  • Enhanced oxygen levels for aquatic life
  • Reduced algae and weed growth
  • Better fish habitat and health
  • Elimination of foul odors
  • Prevention of fish kills

Aeration Systems:

  • Surface aerators for large areas
  • Subsurface diffusers for deep water
  • Fountain aerators for aesthetic appeal
  • Windmill aerators for remote locations
  • Custom aeration solutions
