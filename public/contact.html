Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How quickly can you respond to a quote request?

We typically respond to quote requests within 24 hours during business days. For urgent situations, we offer emergency services with immediate response times.

Do you provide free consultations?

Yes, we offer free consultations and site assessments. Our team will visit your property to evaluate your specific needs and provide a detailed quote.

What information do I need to provide for a quote?

Basic information includes your contact details, property address, water body size, current issues, and preferred timeline. Photos of your pond or lake are also helpful.

How do you handle scheduling and project timing?

We work with your schedule to minimize disruption. Most projects can be completed within 1-3 days depending on size and complexity. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

Do you offer maintenance contracts?

Yes, we offer various maintenance programs including monthly, seasonal, and annual contracts to keep your pond or lake in optimal condition year-round.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, and major credit cards. Payment terms are typically due upon completion of services, with larger projects requiring a deposit.
