Truxor T50 Capabilities

Professional amphibious equipment for comprehensive pond and lake management

Cut

Hydraulic cutting tools for applications both on land and on the water. Cattails, aquatic weeds, grasses, reeds, cane, lotus, lilies! Take it all down on land and in the water.

Collect

Grapple, baskets, and bucket help make the Truxor the best machine on the water. Seamless operation on land and water leveraging interchangeable tools to collect, clear, clean and remove vegetation and debris.

Dig & Dredge

Dredging equipment offers another level of performance and usability, pumping mud, muck and sediment without draining. Perfect for precision dredging of ponds, lakes, wetlands, fish or duck camps.

Aeration

Complete water quality management with professional aeration systems for healthy pond and lake ecosystems.

Advanced Treatment Methods

Comprehensive pond and lake management using chemical and biological solutions

Chemical Application

We are committed to minimizing chemical usage while maintaining effective control. Our Truxor machines work in harmony with natural ecosystem balance, providing selective herbicide applications, bacteria enhancements, pond dyes, and UV reflection treatments when necessary.

We collaborate with licensed applicators to ensure safe and effective treatment. For clients preferring alternative methods, we offer comprehensive mechanical and biological treatment options.

  • Selective herbicide applications
  • Bacteria enhancements
  • Pond dyes and UV treatments
  • Licensed applicator collaboration
  • Eco-friendly alternatives available

Biological Solutions

Harness the power of nature with our biological treatment options. We offer Grass Carp, Tilapia, and Koi stocking services tailored to your specific lake or pond requirements.

Our fish stocking services provide natural aquatic ecosystem management, reducing vegetation growth while maintaining water quality and supporting a balanced aquatic environment.

  • Grass Carp for vegetation control
  • Tilapia for algae management
  • Koi for aesthetic enhancement
  • Custom stocking recommendations
  • Ecosystem balance maintenance
