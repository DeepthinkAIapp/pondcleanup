Our Story

Founded with a passion for preserving Utah's beautiful water bodies, PondCleanup.com has grown from a small local operation to Utah's premier pond and lake cleanup service. Our journey began with a simple mission: to help property owners maintain healthy, beautiful aquatic environments.

Over the years, we've invested in the latest technology and equipment, including the industry-leading Truxor T50, to ensure we can handle any pond or lake maintenance challenge. Our commitment to environmental stewardship and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of hundreds of property owners across Utah.

Today, we continue to lead the industry with innovative solutions, professional expertise, and unwavering dedication to quality service. Every project we undertake reflects our core values of excellence, environmental responsibility, and customer care.
