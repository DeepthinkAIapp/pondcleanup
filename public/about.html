Our Story

Founded with a passion for preserving Utah's beautiful water bodies, PondCleanup.com has grown from a small local operation to Utah's premier pond and lake management specialists. Our journey began with a simple mission: to help property owners maintain healthy, beautiful water features while protecting the environment.

Over the years, we've invested in the most advanced equipment available, including the professional Truxor T50 amphibious machines, and built a team of certified professionals dedicated to excellence in aquatic vegetation control and pond maintenance.

Today, we serve communities throughout Utah, maintaining hundreds of ponds and lakes while building lasting relationships with property owners who trust us with their most valuable water features.
